A welcome reception, social dinner and a one afternoon excursion are planned and included in the conference fee.

Pub Crawl

Participants arriving in Ghent on Sunday the 21st can join us in a leisurely walk in the city centre starting at 7pm. We will introduce you to the local pubs and their beverages. Please note that this walk is not part of the official conference program and expenses are not included in the registration fee.

Welcome Reception

A welcome reception will be held in the Ghent Opera House on Monday evening the 22nd starting at 6pm.

Excursion

Wednesday afternoon will be free for social activities; two group activities are scheduled and included in the conferences fees:
  • - Excursion 1: Historical walk with beer tasting

    • A local guide will take you on a (walking) tour of Ghent's beautiful center, visiting some of the important historical landmarks of the medieval city. The walk will end with a visit to the city brewery and a tasting of the 3 "Gruut"ù beer variants.

    Duration of the walk & beer tasting: ~3h

  • - Excursion 2: Historical walk with library visit and aperitif

    • A local guide will take you on a (walking) tour of Ghent's beautiful center, visiting some of the important historical landmarks of the medieval city including a visit to the magnificent library of the Augustijnen monastery. The walk will end in "Het Vleeshuis"ù with an aperitif.

    Duration of the walk & aperitif: ~3h

    The guided tours start at 14h, meeting place to be determined.

Social Dinner

On Thursday the 25th, the last evening of the workshop, a social dinner will take place at the Monasterium PoortAckere starting at 7:30pm.
